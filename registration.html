Course Details:

  • Course: BSC (Bachelor of Science)
    Bachelor of Science (BSC) is an undergraduate degree program typically lasting 3 to 4 years. It covers a wide range of scientific disciplines and allows students to gain a foundational understanding of various scientific subjects.

    Syllabus:

    • C programming language
    • C++ programming language
    • Java
    • Pl/SQL , etc..
  • Course: MSC (Master of Science)
    Master of Science (MSC) is a postgraduate degree program that allows students to specialize in a specific scientific field. It usually requires 2 years of study and involves advanced coursework and research in the chosen area of science.

    Syllabus:

    • Data mining
    • Software testing
    • Visual basics
    • php , etc..
  • Course: BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
    Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate degree program focused on computer science and applications. It typically spans 3 years and covers topics like programming, software development, and computer systems.

    Syllabus:

    • Introduction to Programming (C/C++)
    • Data Structures and Algorithms
    • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
    • Operating Systems
    • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
    • Software Engineering , etc..
  • Course: MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
    Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a postgraduate degree program in computer science. It typically requires 2 to 3 years of study and includes advanced coursework, project work, and software development.

    Syllabus:

    • Advanced Programming (Java, Python)
    • Data Structures and Algorithms
    • Computer Networks
    • Artificial Intelligence
    • Machine Learning
    • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
    • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
    • Software Engineering
    • Operating Systems
    • Cloud Computing
    • Software Project Management , etc..
  • Course: BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
    Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is an undergraduate degree program in business and management. It typically spans 3 years and provides a foundational understanding of business principles and management concepts.

    Syllabus:

    • Principles of Management
    • Financial Accounting
    • Marketing Management
    • Business Communication
    • Human Resource Management
    • Business Ethics
    • Entrepreneurship , etc..
  • Course: MBA (Master of Business Administration)
    Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate degree program in business and management. It typically spans 1 to 2 years and offers advanced coursework, specialization options, and leadership training.

    Syllabus:

    • Principles of Management
    • Financial Accounting
    • Marketing Management
    • Business Communication
    • Human Resource Management
    • Business Ethics
    • Entrepreneurship , etc..
